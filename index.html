  • FREESTYLE UNDERGROUND STREET DANCERS

    FUSD stands for Freestyle Underground Street Dancers. We are a dance group that strives to teach and spread the culture of various types of "urban" dances to our fellow Aggies, such as popping and bboying. We do NOT hold auditions for individuals interested in being a member of our group. However, we DO have auditions for our Performance Squad which consists of a 45sec prepared piece and a 30sec freestyle to a random song decided by the panel of judges. Regardless, all of our members dance to express ourselves through dance and celebrate the hip-hop culture. Make no mistake, though; although we may not be performance-based, when we do perform, we put it down.

  • MISSION & PURPOSE

    Our mission is to teach about hip-hop culture and freestyle dance, fill the void for Urban Freestyle Dancers on the Texas A&M campus, and to provide a place for members to network and interact together.

    Our purpose is to promote diversity in the Bryan/College Station area through various styles of underground dance. Also, FUSD shall provide an outlet for personal expression through dance. We will also perform our dance styles to raise money for local charities so that local youth understand that there are alternatives, like underground dance, to common destructive activities seen in today's young population.
